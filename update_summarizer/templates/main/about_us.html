Our Story

Summarizing, or writing a summary, entails providing a concise overview of the main points of a text in your own words. The original text is always much shorter than a summary. Update summarizer provides you with a "Summary text tool" to help you summarize and analyze your argumentative texts, articles, scientific texts, history texts, and well-structured analyses work of art.

This educational tool identifies and summarizes the important concepts and information in your documents. Summarize in one click, go to the main idea, or skim through to quickly interpret your texts and develop your syntheses.

Our Mission

As fellow graduate students at East West University(EWU) in 2022, Nahid, Marwa and Rafsan noticed a shift in the way people's need of update summarization. In a modern life consumers are supposed to get information continously to prevent or protect the future Obstruction.

From this shift, this project was born: Update Summarizer. It was founded on "Capstone", the notion that people don't want to be interrupted for not having any tools like this â€” they want to be helped.

Today, the Update Summarizer continues to empower businesses around the world to stop obstacles, start helping, and return their focus to the customer.
